            Help

            Selecting Ships

            • Use the Left Mouse Button to select different ships
            • Clicking on a ship icon at the top of the screen selects that ship and focuses it

            Camera Controls

            • Double clicking LMB or pressing F toggles camera focus on the selected ship
            • Using the Arrow Keys or moving the mouse near the edge of the screen will move the camera in the desired direction

            Ship Movement and Controls

            • Clicking the Auto-steer button disables auto-steer, allowing you to turn and set speed manually by pressing the below buttons
              • W and S increase and decrease speed setting of selected ship
              • A and D turn the ship left and right
            • Clicking the Auto-fire button disables auto-fire, allowing you to fire guns manually by pressing the fire button and then the target location when the guns are reloaded

            Aircraft Camera Controls (To use with focusing)

            • 1 selects the 1st squadron of the selected carrier
            • 2 selects the 2nd, etc.
            • ` returns the selection to the carrier

            Other

            • H toggles this help screen
            • J hides the GUI
            • K hides the grid
            • Press P to pause the game

            Press Q to end the game and go to the home screen
